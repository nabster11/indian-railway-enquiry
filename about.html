About This App

What is this app?

This app was developed with an aim to bring time table of all the reserved, un-reserved and suburban trains search under one roof.

Features

  1. List of stations
  2. Trains between stations
  3. Route of a train
  4. Current live status of the train
  5. PNR current status.
  6. Fare of the class

    7. ... and much more to come
